## New York City Government Job Dataset Insights

The dataset we explored is one of New York City government job postings, providing  information on roles, hiring agencies, salaries, and work locations. 

### Key Data Takeaways
1. *Salary Distribution:* The vast majority of positions feature salaries under $300,000.

2. *Centralized Hubs:* 6% of work locations are concentrated at 100 Gold Street, the headquarters for the Department of Housing Preservation and Development.

3. *Data Completeness:* 37% of the 'preferred qualifications' fields contain missing data, indicating potential reporting gaps.

4. *Primary Hirers:* The Department of Environmental Protection and the Department of Design and Construction are the city's most active hiring agencies.

5. *Residency Mandates:* 45% of NYC municipal jobs require employees to establish residency within the city within 90 days of their appointment.
